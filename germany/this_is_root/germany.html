Germany

A Cultural and Economic Powerhouse

My favourite country is Germany. Located in the heart of Europe, and ressponsible for many amazing inventions, architecture, beer, and a lot more great things. I will try to explain Germany in this article, made for a school project.

1. History

Germany has played a huge role in both world wars in the 20th century, and it has a really interesting and engaging history. After the fall of the Berlin Wall in 1989, ending the Cold War, Germany reunited and overcame the challenges of a divided nation. Today, Germany is one of the largest beer producers in the world and a huge player in many industries and on the global stage in general, and has joined the United Nations, EU, and NATO.

2. Geography

Here are a few facts about the geography of Germany:

a. Germany is the seventh (7th) largest country in Europe, with a land mass of 357,022 km² (357K).
b. Germany borders Denmark to the north, Poland and the Czech Republic to the east, Austria to the southeast, and Switzerland to the southwest.
c. The highest mountain in Germany, the Zugspitze, has a highest point of 2,962 metres. That is almost 3 times higher than Hungary's highest point, Mount Kékes, topping at 1,014 metres. (~2.92 times higher | 2962 / 1014)
